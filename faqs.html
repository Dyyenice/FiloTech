Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a question about the car rental? Browse the following topics or click one of the questions to find an answer and set out!

How can I do my car reservation?

After you have completed the relevant selections in the Booking Reservation section, you can make your car reservation using your Visa / MasterCard credit cards. In addition, you can also reservation with our 444 4 444 (YES) call center or our offices.

Do you have a payment option outside the credit card?

No, we cannot accept the payment outside of Visa / MasterCard credit cards, the debit card is not accepted. No sales channel can be made from our cash, deed, debit card or virtual card with vehicle booking

I don't have a credit card / My card limit is insufficient, can I pay by credit card for another?

By our safety rules, the card information is required to match the driver information for car rental services made through all of our reservation channels.

Can I pay in the office?

If you perform your reservation with our 444 4 444 call center or offices, you can pay in the office. The reservations on our website are not available in the office in the office. If you book early, your reservation will be more advantageous.

How can I pick up the vehicle I booked?

The offices of our guests are communicated at the moment of booking information. Simply go to our relevant office with your booking information. We ask you to have your driver's license and credit card (2 pieces for top segment vehicles).

What are your Cancel / Return Terms?

If you report your cancellation request to us on the date you booked, all the amount you pay will be returned to your card. Our Refund application in your other cancellation requests are as follows: - Returns after the interruption of 3-day leasing fee, if the vehicle is not received less than 24 hours to the receiving time. If your reservation is less than 3 days, it cannot be returned. - If there is more than 24 hours of reception time to the reception time, the lease is returned after the deduction is made. - Free cancellation and returns are made if you purchase the assurance package. For car rental process rules, you can review the Enterprise Rental Agreement details.
